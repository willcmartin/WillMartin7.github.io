Papers

The Matrix Calculus You Need For Deep Learning (Terence Parr and Jeremy Howard, 2018)
  A great resource that I have been using while building pocketnet. The authors start from the fundamentals and build their way to vector calc. and its use in backpropagation, gradient descent, etc.

Blog posts

Alien Dreams: An Emerging Art Scene (Charlie Snell, 2021)
  A great coverage of some recent AI projects for generating art. I played around with the VQ-GAN+CLIP sample notebook for awhile and had quite a lot of fun.

Book Cover Trends thru Time (via Dune) (Lauren Panepinto, 2020)
  This blog post led me on an eBay chase to get the "correct" Dune cover. Someday I will need to do the same for The Lord of the Rings.

A Survival Guide to a PhD (Andrej Karpathy, 2016)
  Hey, you! Let me into a PhD program so I can make use of the only blog post with "Survial Guide" in the title that actually seems useful.

A Recipe for Training Neural Networks (Andrej Karpathy, 2019)
  Everywhere I go, people are recommending this guide. I may as well pass it on.

Books

The Metamorphosis of Prime Intellect (Roger Williams, 1994)
  This sci-fi book is incredibly gruesome, but it brings up some interesting discussions on the future of AGI and humans.
